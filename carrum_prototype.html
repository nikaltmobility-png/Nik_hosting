Common questions
Is my ₹5,000 safe? ▾
Yes! Your ₹5,000 is fully refundable if you don't continue at the hub — returned in 5–7 working days.
What happens after I pay? ▾
Our team confirms your hub visit. Bring your documents and you'll get started with a vehicle on the same day.
When will I get the vehicle? ▾
You get the vehicle on the day of your hub visit after document verification — usually within 24–48 hrs.
What documents are needed? ▾
Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Commercial Driving License, Uber Partner App ID, and passport size photo.
